About Van High TPUSA

Turning Point USA chapters support student engagement, host speakers, and run events that promote free speech, civic involvement, and leadership. Van High’s chapter is student-run and open to all students.

Mission

Encourage civic involvement and educate students on economics and civics through respectful debate and community events.
